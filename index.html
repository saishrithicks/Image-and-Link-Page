GOJO vs SUKUNA

Satoru Gojo vs. Sukuna, also known as The Battle of the Strongest, is the fight between the strongest sorcerer of today, Satoru Gojo, and the strongest sorcerer in history, Ryomen Sukuna. It takes place on December 24, 2018, in the abandoned Makyo Shinjuku to decide the future of the jujutsu world.
